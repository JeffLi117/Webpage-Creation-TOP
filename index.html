Detroit style pizza is a crispy rectangular pizza traditionally topped with Wisconsin brick cheese and a tomato-based sauce.
Chicago style pizza is a deep-dish pizza named for being cooked in a pan and stuffed with delicious ingredients.
Neapolitan pizza is usually baked for 60-90 seconds in a wood-fired oven. The dough, short time, and high temperature lend itself to be softer and more elastic.
